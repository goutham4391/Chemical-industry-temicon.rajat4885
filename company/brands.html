Our brands

The three brands temilux®, temiLife® and holotools® reflect temicon’s comprehensive expertise in micro- and nano-structuring:

  • innovative optical LED lighting elements in the temilux® family
  • temiLife® applications in biotechnology,medicine and pharmacy
  • large-surface, seamless embossing tools from holotools®

Our brands: temiLux®, temiLife® and holotools®

Threw strong innovation, temicon has established its own brands in many fields of application. temiLux®, temiLife® and holotools® show the many applications for micro- and nanostructured functional surfaces.

 

 

Temilux®

temilux®ID is for invisible deglaring for achieving standards-compliant office lighting.

Holotools

holotools® is the brand for large-surface, seamless structuring. The products can be refined as embossing tools or injection molding inserts.

temiLife

temiLife® stands for medical components that can be manufactured in-house and mass-produced.
